About me

My name is Aneesh and I am from Warangal. I am a 3rd-year student at KL University and I belong to a nuclear family. I am passionate about learning new technologies and I am a full-stack web developer certified in Azure Fundamentals.

I work with various programming languages such as Python, Java, and C++. I also work with different technologies such as Spring Boot, Django, MySQL, MongoDB, and OpenStreetMap.
